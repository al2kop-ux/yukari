Blog Content Audit
Semantic SEO, NLP entity optimisation, query fan-outs, E-E-A-T

Semantic SEO Principle: Entity-First Content for YMYL Medical Sites

Google's NLP (Natural Language Processing) evaluates pages based on entities (people, places, concepts, drugs, medical terms) and their relationships. For weight loss content, every page should densely cover the relevant entity cluster: GLP-1, semaglutide, tirzepatide, MHRA, BMI, insulin resistance, caloric deficit, satiety hormones (GLP-1, GIP, leptin, ghrelin), NICE guidelines. Current blogs appear to lack this entity density, limiting topical authority.
